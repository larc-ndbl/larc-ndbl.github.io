Welcome to Our Neurodiversity Book Collection!

Distributed by the Los Altos Rotary Satellite Club -- Neurodiversity Book List

Every child deserves to see themselves in the stories they read. Our Neurodiversity Book Drive is dedicated to increasing access to books that celebrate and reflect the experiences of neurodivergent children. From autism and ADHD to dyslexia and sensory processing, these thoughtfully selected books help foster understanding, empathy, and inclusion in schools and community spaces.

This project is proudly sponsored by the Los Altos Rotary Satellite Club in honor of Neurodivergent Awareness Month, April 2025.

We hope this collection inspires young readers, families, and educators to embrace and celebrate the beauty of diverse minds.
