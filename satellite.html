Los Altos Rotary Satellite Club

We're a small, hands-on team within the broader Los Altos Rotary family, created for people who want to serve the community with a more flexible rhythm. As part of an established club, we benefit from a welcoming network, mentorship, and the ability to take on meaningful local projects right away.

Our meeting cadence is designed for busy schedules. Instead of weekly noon meetings, we gather once a month and stay connected in between through online updates and a WhatsApp group. This helps members participate consistently while balancing work, family, and life.

We focus on projects that make a visible difference close to home. A recent example: curating and distributing inclusive, neurodiversity-affirming books to local schools and Little Free Libraries. We aim for efforts that are thoughtful, inclusive, and practical—projects you can feel proud to be part of.

Our members come from a wide mix of backgrounds—healthcare, education, tech, business, communications, and more. What unites us is a shared desire to put skills to work for good, learn from one another, and create a welcoming space for all who want to contribute.

Because we're growing, there's room to help shape what comes next: which projects we prioritize, how we meet, and the culture we build together. If you're looking for a way to get involved that fits your life—and lets you make a real impact—we'd love to meet you.

To learn more, see our Welcome page on ClubRunner

If you're curious about membership or upcoming projects, reach out at larc-satellite@googlegroups.com or come visit a monthly meeting. There's a place for you here.
